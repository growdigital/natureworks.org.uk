Back to buffering my social media messages!

Saturday 28 January 2023

I have been trying to write a little something every day, somehow related to forest gardening or the work I do.

It has been quite draining of late, less gardening, more admin. Tomorrow, I will be out in the garden, clearing up the polytunnel for the spring.

With work related stuff, I‘ve been uploading a lot of client videos on my YouTube channel. These really are work notes in video form; I’m doing them anyway and upload them for general interest.

I‘ve also started using Bulk Buffer, a service that works with Buffer, to mass upload messages to my Facebook and Twitter accounts. I interact almost exclusively on Mastodon now mas.to/@natureworks but still autopost stuff to The Other Places just to keep visibility really.
