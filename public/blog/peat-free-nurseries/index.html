Does your nursery use peat-free compost? A list of nurseries that do, compiled by Nic Wilson 🙏

Wednesday 18 September 2019

Using peat-based compost contributes to #ClimateBreakdown and wildlife habitat loss. Best thing you can do is use a peat-free compost (such as Sylvagrow, FertileFibre, Dalefoot etc) and buy your plants from a nursery that use peat-free compost.

Nic Wilson @dogwooddays_nic has put together a list of peat-free nurseries here:

dogwooddays.net/2019/09/18/save-our-rainforests-the-peat-free-nurseries-list/
