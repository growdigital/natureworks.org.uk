A handy list of the Roots and All podcasts

Thursday 6 February 2020

Sarah Wilson put out a review of all past Roots and All podcasts & budcasts. I like a good list, so I typed them all up. Halfway through, I realised how many there are but it was too late to give up.

Subscribe to podcast on Apple or Android, or via your favourite podcast software. There is even an episode on, ahem, forest gardening 😎.

  • New to gardening
    • Plant profiles
      • Alliums ep.8, with Jackie Currie
      • Daffodils ep.19, with Adrian Scamp
      • Peonies ep.33, with Alec White
      • Ivy ep.26, with Angela Tansy
      • Dahlias ep.1, with Dave Gillam
    • Allotments Part 1, ep.5 & Allotments Part 2, budcast
    • RHS Plant hardiness, budcast
  • Working with a garden designer
    • Hiring a Gardener ep.2, with Claire Vokins (see How Much Should You Be Paying A Gardener?)

  • House plants
    • Houseplants ep.6, with Jason Stevens
    • Carnivorous Plants, budcast, Matthew Soper

    • Plants as Art, budcast, with Alyson Mowat

    • Vegan Lifestyle ep.14, with Cleve West
    • Vegan Gardening ep.15, with Matthew Appleby
  • Vegetable growing
    • Stock Free Farming pt1 & pt2, budcast, with Iain Tolhurst
