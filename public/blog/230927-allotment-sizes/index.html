A full allotment is 250m²

Wednesday 27 September 2023

Just a quick note to myself, according to The National Allotment Society, a UK allotment is 10 poles, which is 250m².

  • Full: 250m², approx 16m x 16m
  • Half: 125m², approx 11m x 11m
  • Quarter: 63m², approx 8m x 8m
