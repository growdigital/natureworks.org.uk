Recommended books, suppliers, websites and podcasts. Saving you time and enlarging your brain 🧠

Books

  • Creating a Forest Garden by Martin Crawford
  • A New Garden Ethic by Benjamin Vogt, the morality of gardening with natives
  • The Wild Garden, by Violet Stevenson, 1985 but pretty much the only book covering the use of UK native plants
  • Around the world in 80 plants by Stephen Barstow, a global romp around edible plants
  • The Garden of Equal Delights by Anni Kelsey, forest gardening from the gardener’s perspective, delightful and intriguing

Suppliers

  • Backyard Larder, Alison Tindale
  • Incredible Veg, Mandy Barber
  • Agroforestry Research Trust, Martin Crawford
  • Cool Temperate, Phil Corbett
  • Future Forests, for hedging

Websites

  • Plants For A Future brilliant database of useful plants, essential
  • Database of Insects & their Food intoxifying amount of info about which plants are hosts for which insects
  • orangepippin.com for information on fruit varieties
  • RHS Plant Finder, with checkbox for native plants filter
  • fruitID fruit ID website!
  • Edimentals.com by Stephen Barstow
  • Edimentals Facebook group
  • Forest Garden UK Facebook group

Podcasts

  • Roots and All best wildlife gardening podcast out there, with Sarah Wilson
  • Growing Greener best US wildlife gardening podcast, with Thomas Christopher
  • A Way To Garden with Margaret Roach: long running, always interesting
  • Garden Organic increasingly vegan, increasingly perennial
